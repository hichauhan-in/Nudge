Nudge! — Privacy Policy

100% On-device · Offline · No trackers

Effective date: 16 July 2026 · Last updated: 16 July 2026

The short version: Nudge! is a digital‑wellbeing app that works entirely on your device. It does not collect, sell, or transmit your personal information. Your list of guarded apps, usage timers, session history, and settings never leave your phone. There are no accounts, no ads, and no third‑party analytics or trackers.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Nudge! mobile application (in.hichauhan.nudge, the “App”, “we”, “us”) treats your information, in the interest of full transparency about how we handle sensitive user and device data. By installing or using the App, you agree to this policy.

1 Summary of our approach

No personal data collected No internet transmission by us No accounts No ads No analytics / trackers All data stays on your device

Nudge! helps you use selected apps more intentionally by prompting you to set a mindful time limit when you open them. Everything needed to do this happens locally on your device. We do not operate any server that receives your data.

2 Information we collect

We do not collect or process any personally identifiable information. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or account credentials.

The App creates and stores the following information only on your device, and only to make the App work:

  • Your monitored‑app selections — the package names (e.g. com.instagram.android) of the apps you choose to guard.
  • Session & usage data — timers you set, and a local history of interventions (e.g. limits completed, extended, or ignored) used to show your on‑device statistics.
  • App settings & preferences — such as your timer‑behavior mode, sarcastic mode, notification and appearance preferences.

This information is stored in the App’s private, sandboxed storage (local database and preferences) and is never uploaded anywhere by us.

3 How the Accessibility Service is used

To know when you open a guarded app, Nudge! uses Android’s Accessibility Service. We want to be completely clear about its scope:

  • The service listens only for window/app‑switch events to read the package name of the app currently in the foreground.
  • It is used solely to check whether that package is one you asked us to guard, so it can show the mindful prompt.
  • Nudge! does not read, log, record, or transmit the contents of your screen, your keystrokes, your messages, your passwords, or your browsing/search activity.
  • All processing happens on‑device and in real time; nothing from the Accessibility Service is stored or shared.

5 Data storage & retention

All App data is stored locally on your device for as long as the App is installed. Because we never receive your data, we have nothing to retain on our side. You are always in control:

  • Use Configure → Wipe Intercept Logs inside the App to clear your usage history at any time.
  • Remove individual apps from monitoring in the Monitor Console.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all of its locally stored data from your device.

6 Data sharing & selling

We do not share, rent, or sell your information with anyone, because we never collect it in the first place. There is no advertising SDK, no analytics SDK, and no third‑party tracking in the App.

7 Third‑party services

Nudge! is distributed through Google Play and uses a small piece of Google infrastructure:

  • Google Play In‑App Review — the App may ask Google Play to show its standard “rate this app” dialog. This flow is handled entirely by Google Play; Nudge! does not receive your rating, review text, or Google account details. Any processing is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play services / Play Store — used only for app distribution and updates, subject to Google’s policies.

Optional convenience links in the App (for example, a voluntary support/donation link or the developer’s website) simply open your own browser or payment app; any activity there is governed by that third party’s own privacy policy, not ours.

8 Children’s privacy

Nudge! is a general‑audience productivity tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Since no data is collected, no children’s data is processed.

9 Security

Your data stays within the App’s private, OS‑protected storage on your device. Because nothing is transmitted to or stored on external servers by us, the common risks associated with cloud data breaches do not apply to information handled by Nudge!.

10 Your rights & choices

As we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data of yours for us to access, correct, export, or delete on a server. You retain full control of all on‑device data through the in‑app controls and by uninstalling the App, as described in Section 5.

11 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
